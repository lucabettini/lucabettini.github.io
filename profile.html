Born 24 years ago in the venetian countryside, I started messing around with computers at a very young age, connecting to the internet for the first time when I was 7, ADSL did not exist (not in my area at least) and 56k modem made that half beautiful half ghastly sound.

After 6 years of continuous sperimentation - during which I created an online forum, discovered the wonders of Linux and taught myself the basics of programming with Python - I chose to keep computer science a hobby and to dedicate myself to another great passion of mine, the humanities.

In 2019 I obtained a first class degree in History at the University of Venice, and by that time I was ready to go back to my roots. I started coding again, built a powerful computer and enrolled in the project-driven web development course at start2impact, to become a full-stack developer.
